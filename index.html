About

Myself

Born in Bayamon, Puerto Rico and raised in Utuado, Puerto Rico. I identify as first generation immigrant from a colonized island, proud Puertorican. I am first and foremost a barista, having worked in the industry for over 17 years. Aspiring polymath (means I love knowledge) and entrepreneur, I have decided to organize myself with this website to showcase some projects I have worked on, business' that I own and am working on launching in the near future.


Happily married my best friend and partner in 2017. Currently live in the greatest city in the world: New York City. I have a passion for social justice, equal rights and basic human rights for everyone no matter what race, religion or sexual orientation.


I am a full stack web developer, working freelance in my off time. Help manage a bulk liquid transportation company with my wife and work for Califia Farms as a sales rep in NYC. Have a few websites that I own that are under deveoplment and hope to have them launched in the next few months.
